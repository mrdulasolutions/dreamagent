# NNN. Short imperative title

- **Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Superseded by [ADR-XXX]
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Deciders:** name(s) or "the project"

## Context

What problem are we solving? What forces are at play?

## Decision

The choice we made, in one paragraph.

## Consequences

What becomes easier? What becomes harder? What do we accept as a tradeoff?

## Alternatives Considered

Numbered list with one line per alternative and why we didn't pick it.

## Related

Links to issues, PRs, prior ADRs, external references.
